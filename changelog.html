Every correction to a published figure or claim, and every material change to how data is presented, logged with what changed and why. This is the record we're accountable to.

Corrections are logged here regardless of direction — whether a fix made a trend look better or worse than previously stated. Per-chart "data verified current as of" timestamps (visible under each chart) track routine data refreshes; this page tracks factual and methodological corrections plus significant site changes.
